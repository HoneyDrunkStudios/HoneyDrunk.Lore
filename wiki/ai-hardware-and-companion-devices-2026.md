## HoneyDrunk implications
- Track this as a possible future runtime/client surface for agents, not as an immediate build target.
- HoneyDrunk should keep agent UX portable across phone, desktop, web, voice, and future companion-device surfaces; avoid betting on a single proprietary hardware path.
- If real specs emerge, evaluate permissions, local/cloud data handling, tool invocation, developer SDKs, and whether agents can run HoneyDrunk workflows safely.
