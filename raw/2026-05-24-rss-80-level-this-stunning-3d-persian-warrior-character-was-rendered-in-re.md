# This Stunning 3D Persian Warrior Character Was Rendered in Real-Time Using UE5

Source: https://80.lv/articles/this-stunning-3d-persian-warrior-character-was-rendered-in-real-time-using-ue5/

Stephanie Almogabar Junior Editor 24 May 2026 This Stunning 3D Persian Warrior Character Was Rendered in Real-Time Using UE5 # Character Art # News # 3ds Max # Unreal Engine # Marmoset Toolbag # Marvelous Designer # Photoshop # ZBrush # Substance 3D Painter This highly detailed Persian Warrior character is real-time rendered in Unreal Engine 5. What kind of stories do you picture her in?
Ju Hwan Jeon, 3D Character Modeler, shared a stunning Persian Female Warrior project on his ArtStation .
He explained that his goal was to reimagine ancient Persian attire with modern ornaments, keeping a strong and elegant appearance.
He used Unreal Engine 5 to render the character in real-time, and other software like 3ds Max, ZBrush, Substance 3D Painter, Marmoset Toolbag, Marvelous Designer, and Photoshop were used in her creation.
