🔐 ARC Terminal Breakdown
What Exactly Is This Project?

To put it simply in one sentence:
@TheARCTERMINAL aims to be a "cryptographically verifiable ChatGPT" not just an AI that you have to trust.

The problem they aim to solve is:
Currently, when using ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini or other AI agents, we have to trust that:

~ Our data isn’t stored
~ The model being used is accurate
~ The AI’s output isn’t manipulated
~ The agent follows instructions according to the rules

We can’t verify any of that.
ARC is trying to shift the paradigm:

Trust → Proof
or
Trust us → Prove it to us

A Simple Analogy

Currently:
✅ The lawyer says they’ll delete the document after reading it

But...

❌ You have no proof

ARC wants to create a system like this:
✅ The document is encrypted
✅ Processed by AI
✅ There is cryptographic proof that the data isn’t stored
✅ There is proof of the model used
✅ There is proof of the actions taken
✅ Anyone can verify this proof

Without having to trust ARC
