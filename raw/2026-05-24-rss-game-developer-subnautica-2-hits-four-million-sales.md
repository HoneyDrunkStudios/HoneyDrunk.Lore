1 Min Read Via Unknown Worlds Newsbrief: Subnautica 2 , launched on May 14th in Early Access, has hit four million sales. Gematsu reports that a cool two million copies were sold in the first 12 hours of launch. 
The site quotes a press release from developer Untold Worlds, with executive Producer Fernando Melo noting "We're incredibly grateful for the response from players around the world... Seeing millions of players dive into this new world during Early Access has been an incredible moment for the team. Community feedback continues to help shape the future of Subnautica 2 , and we're excited to keep building the experience together with our players." 
The game is currently sitting at "very positive" reviews on Steam, and launched successfully despite a high profile legal dispute between ousted Unknown Worlds leadership and Krafton.
